Book Website¶

Welcome to the companion website for the first edition of Write Your First Program published by Prentice Hall India Learning Private Limited. The website attempts to supplement the book by providing Chapter Companions and Additional Articles, while also being a home for the book on the Internet.

Discussion Forum¶

A Google group is available for discussing the book’s materials and related programming topics not necessarily limited to C and Python. You can post your questions and doubts here. The questions will be answered either by me or other readers like you.

You may also suggest any topic which you want me to cover in a future article on this website.
